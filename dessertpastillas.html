No-cook Caramel Pastillas

Made with dulce de leche and powdered milk, these No-Cook Caramel Pastillas are seriously addictive. Soft, chewy, and delicious, they're quick and easy for anytime cravings.

prep time

20 mins

serving

8 servings

Tags : Dessert

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, combine dulce de leche, full cream milk and peanuts. With a spoon, stir together until well-combined. Refrigerate for about 10 to 15 minutes to firm up a bit for easier handling.

step 2

Scoop about ½ teaspoon and roll into smooth balls about ½-inch diameter. Or roll into 1-inch long logs. Roll pastillas in sugar until fully coated.

step 3

Wrap each pastillas with a piece of white paper (resulting into a "tube") and then wrap with the colored Japanese paper. Insert the sides of the Japanese paper into the white paper "tube" to completely seal pastillas.

ingredients

1 can (14 ounces) dulce de leche

1 3/4 cups powdered full cream milk

1/4 cup dry roasted peanuts, chopped

1/2 cup sugar

tools

Serving Cups

Measuring Cup/Spoon

colored Japanese paper, cut into 2-inch squares

White paper, cut into 1-1/2 inch squares
