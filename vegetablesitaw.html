Adobong Sitaw

Adobong Sitaw is a vegetable dish composed of string beans cooked adobo style. As you might have noticed, almost all ingredients can be cooked using the famous adobo style â€“ be it meat or vegetables.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

25 mins

serving

3 to 4 servings

Tags : vegetable pork

instructions

step 1

Heat a frying pan or wok then sear the pork.

step 2

When oil and juice comes out of the pork, add garlic and onions then cook for 2 minutes.

step 3

Pour-in the soy sauce, vinegar, and water then bring to a boil.

step 4

Shake-in the ground black pepper and stir.

step 5

Cover and simmer for 15 minutes or until the pork is tender.

step 6

Add the string beans and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.

step 7

Turn off the heat and transfer to a serving bowl.

step 8

Share and enjoy!

ingredients

1 lb string beans sitaw, cut in 2 inch length

1/4 lb pork belly thinly sliced (this one is optional)

1/2 cup vinegar

1/2 cup soy sauce

1 pc. onion thinly sliced lengthwise

4 cloves of garlic crushed

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 cup water

tools

Frying Pan

Cutting Board

Knife

Measuring Cups / Spoons

Cooking Spoon or Spatula

Lid or Cover

Serving Bowl
