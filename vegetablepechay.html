Sarciadong Pechay

Sarciadong Pechay is one of the recipes that easily comes to mind because all you really need to do is combine a bunch of commonly used ingredients in one pan. That’s really all there is to it! That’s why I can easily recommend this to people who are still figuring out how to move around the kitchen.

prep time

5 mins

cook time

12 mins

serving

3 servings

Tags : vegetable

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a pan. Sauté garlic, onion, and tomatoes.

step 2

Add the pechay. Sauté for 20 seconds and then pour water. Let it boil.

step 3

Gently pour the beaten egg around the pan. Stir and continue cooking until the eggs start to solidify.

step 4

Season with fish sauce and ground black pepper. Serve with rice.

step 5

Share and enjoy!

ingredients

3 bunches pechay

2 eggs

2 tomatoes

5 cloves garlic

1 onion

1 cup water

2 tablespoons cooking oil

Fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste

tools

Mixing Bowl

Cutting Board

Knife

Pan

Whisk

Spatula or wooden spoons

Measuring Spoons

Serving Plate
