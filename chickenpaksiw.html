Chicken Paksiw

If there’s one thing that can be said about Filipino cuisine, it’s that we love sour dishes. Something about this kind of taste simply appeals to us –– think of the classic sinigang, or the homey sinampalukang manok. Perhaps sourness gives us that much-needed refreshing jolt for the day; perhaps we just like to keep our taste buds on edge. But no matter what the reason is, I’m sure that sour food lovers –– and all food lovers, for that matter –– will come to enjoy this delightful chicken paksiw!

prep time

5 mins

cook time

40 mins

serving

4 servings

Tags : Chicken

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a pan. Saute onion and garlic.

step 2

Once the onion softens, add chicken. Stir and saute for 1 minute.

step 3

Pour the lechon sauce into the pan and then add vinegar. Let the mixture boil before stirring.

step 4

Pour water into the pan. Let it boil.

step 5

Add Knorr Chicken Cube, bay leaves, and whole peppercorn. Stir. Cover the pan and then adjust the heat to the lowest setting. Continue cooking for 35 minutes.

step 6

Take the cover off the pan. Continue cooking until the sauce evaporates and reduces to half.

step 7

Add sugar and then season with salt.

step 8

Transfer it to a serving bowl and serve with warm rice. Share and enjoy!

ingredients

1 lb. leftover chicken

1 piece Knorr Chicken Cube

1 cup lechon sauce

5 cloves garlic

1 piece onion

1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

4 pieces dried bay leaves

1/2 cup white vinegar

1/4 cup sugar

1 cup water

Salt to taste

3 tablespoons cooking oil

tools

Knife

Cutting board

Mixing bowls

Large Pot

Ladle

Measuring cups and spoons

Wooden spoon or spatula
