Utan nga Langka

Indulge in the wholesome goodness of our dish today, which is not only tasty and satisfying but also packed with health benefits. So why not take your taste buds on a journey and savour the richness of nature with this Jackfruit dish. Utan ng Langka is a delicious and traditional Filipino dish made with young jackfruit, cooked in creamy coconut milk and fragrant malunggay or bago leaves.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

35 mins

serving

6 servings

Tags : vegetable

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a large wok.

step 2

Saut√© onion and garlic.

step 3

Add the dried fish once the onions soften. Cook for 1 minute.

step 4

Add unripe jackfruit. Cook while stirring for 2 to 3 minutes.

step 5

Add chili peppers and bago leaves. Stir.

step 6

Season with 1 tablespoon fish sauce and then pour-in the coconut milk. Let boil.

step 7

Add vinegar. Cook for 5 to 8 minutes.

step 8

Add Knorr Shrimp Cube. Continue cooking until the liquid evaporates completely.

step 9

Season with ground black pepper and fish sauce as needed.

step 10

Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with warm rice.

step 11

Share and enjoy!

ingredients

2 lbs. unripe jackfruit

1 Knorr Shrimp cube

3 cups coconut milk

2 cups bago leaves

1 cup dried fish

2 onions

5 cloves garlic

3 Thai chili pepper

2 tablespoons vinegar

Fish sauce to taste

3 tablespoons cooking oil

Mixing Bowl

Cutting Board

Knife
