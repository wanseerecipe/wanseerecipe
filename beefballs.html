Cheesy Corned Beef Balls

Tender, meaty, and delicious, corned beef is a favorite breakfast option — not just among Filipinos, but across the globe. Just the sight of this pink-red meat is enough to make the start of any day better and brighter. Whether you’re a kid getting ready for school or an adult about to head out for a long day of work, corned beef is the quick and easy fix that’ll satisfy any stomach.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

20 mins

serving

1 to 2 servings

Tags : beef snacks

instructions

step 1

Combine CDO corned beef, 1 egg, flour, salt, and ground black pepper in a bowl. Mix well until the mixture becomes less sticky. You can add more flour as needed.

step 2

Scoop around 3 tablespoons of the mixture. Stuff the center with mozzarella cheese and then roll the mixture to form a ball-shaped figure.

step 3

Arrange 3 bowls on your working station. Fill the first with 5 tablespoons of flour, the next with 1 beaten egg, and the third bowl with breadcrumbs.

step 4

Dredge the corned beef balls in flour and dip in beaten egg. Roll it in breadcrumbs ensuring that the balls get completely coated.

step 5

Heat cooking oil in a cooking pot. Deep-fry the corned beef balls in a low heat setting until the exterior of the balls turn golden brown.

step 6

Arrange it on a serving plate. Serve. Share and enjoy!

ingredients

450 grams CDO Home Style Corned Beef

1 1/2 cups bread crumbs

2 eggs

4 ounces mozzarella cheese

10 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 1/2 cups cooking oil

Salt and ground black pepper to taste

tools

Mixing Bowl

Spoon

Measuring Cups and Spoons

Deep Fryer or Deep Frying Pan

Slotted Spoon or Tongs
