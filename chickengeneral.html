General Tso Chicken

General Tso Chicken is a popular Asian chicken recipe that goes well with Yang Chow fried rice. This version will certainly tickle your taste buds. If you have more time, try making the fried rice too.

prep time

5 mins

cook time

20 mins

serving

4 servings

Tags : Chicken

instructions

step 1

Combine the marinade ingredients in a large bowl. Stir.

step 2

Add the chicken. Marinate for 1 hour.

step 3

Meanwhile, combine the sauce ingredients in a bowl. Mix well and then set aside.

step 4

Heat the oil in a cooking pot.

step 5

Dredge the marinated chicken in cornstarch and then dip into the beaten egg mixture.

step 6

Roll the chicken back in the cornstarch and then deep fry until the color turns light brown (about 5 to 7 minutes). Do not overcook. Set aside.

step 7

On a clean pan, saut√© onion in 2 tablespoons cooking oil.

step 8

Add the sauce mixture and then stir until the mixture starts to thicken.

step 9

Put-in the deep fried chicken and stir until the sauce coats each piece.

step 10

Add the chopped scallions. Transfer to a serving plate.

step 11

Serve. Share and enjoy!

ingredients

2 lbs. boneless chicken breast

1/2 cup chopped scallions

1 egg

1 cup cornstarch

2 cups cooking oil

1 small onion

Sauce ingredients:

1 1/4 cups chicken broth

6 tablespoons soy sauce

3 tablespoons vinegar

3 tablespoons cornstarch

3 tablespoons water

1/2 cup granulated white sugar

1/4 cup dry sherry

2 teaspoons garlic powder

3 teaspoons minced ginger

Marinade ingredients:

3 tablespoons soy sauce

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

tools

Knife

Cutting board

Mixing bowl
