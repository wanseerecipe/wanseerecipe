Filipino Chicken Pasta Alfredo

The classic chicken pasta alfredo is famous for its creamy and satisfyingly savory quality. It can also be pretty hearty with our yummy fettuccine pasta mixed with bits of chicken. And another notable aspect to this recipe? It’s quite easy to make!

prep time

10 mins

cook time

30 mins

serving

4 servings

Tags : Chicken

instructions

step 1

Cook pasta according to package instructions. Set aside.

step 2

Make the creamy sauce by melting butter in a cooking pot. Sauté onion and garlic until the onion softens.

step 3

Add CDO chicken flakes. Continue cooking for 2 minutes.

step 4

Pour the all-purpose cream into the pot. Let it boil. Adjust the heat to low setting. Continue cooking while stirring occasionally until the sauce thicken to your desired consistency.

step 5

Add parmesan cheese. Stir.

step 6

Put the cooked pasta unto the cooking pot with the sauce. Toss until well blended. Season with chopped parsley.

step 7

Transfer to a pasta bowl. Serve.

step 8

Share and enjoy!

ingredients

300 grams CDO Chicken Flakes in Oil

1 lb. fettucine pasta

500 ml. all-purpose cream

1 onion

3/4 cup Parmesan cheese

3 cloves garlic

2 tablespoons butter

tools

Knife

Cutting board

Mixing bowl

Colander or strainer

large Pot

Wooden spoon or spatula

Measuring cups and spoons

Large Skillet or Pan

Tongs
