Lechon Kawali

Lechon Kawali is a beloved centerpiece on the Filipino table, especially during New Year’s Eve, known for its irresistibly crispy texture! Derived from the Spanish word “lechon,” meaning roasted pork, and “kawali,” the Filipino term for frying pan, lechon kawali refers to pork belly that’s deep-fried.

prep time

2 hrs

cook time

20 mins

serving

4 servings

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

Combine pork belly, water, whole peppercorn and dried bay leaves in a wide cooking pot. Cover and let boil.

step 2

Adjust the heat to a simmer. Continue cooking for 1 ½ to 2 hours.

step 3

Remove the pork from the cooking pot. Place it on a plate and let it cool down.

step 4

Rub Maggi Magic Sarap all over the pork belly.

step 5

Soak it under the sun until the skin completely dries.

step 6

Heat the oil in a wok. Once the oil gets hot, carefully deep fry the pork belly until the outer part becomes golden brown and crispy.

step 7

Remove from the wok and put it over a wire rack. Let it cool down.

step 8

Slice into serving pieces. Serve with lechon sauce. Share and enjoy!

ingredients

3 lbs. pork belly

8 grams Maggi Magic Sarap

6 cups water

1 tablespoon whole peppercorn

5 dried bay leaves

4 cups cooking oil

tools

Large Kawali

Sharp Knife

Chopping Board

Wok

Wire rack

Measuring Cups and Spoons

Tongs or Slotted Spoon
