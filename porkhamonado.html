Pork Hamonado

Filipinos are no stranger to the sweet and savory combination in food. A number of salty dishes from overseas have their own fun Pinoy versions that are usually a bit more honeyed and saccharine. Ever heard of Filipino spaghetti? But the beauty of this culinary flavor duo can be seen in various recipes that came straight from home, such as the meaty and zesty Pork Hamonado.

prep time

5 mins

cook time

1 hr

serving

6 servings

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

Heat the oil in a cooking pot. Fry each side of the pork belly for 2 minutes.

step 2

Pour-in the soy sauce, pineapple juice, and half of the pineapple chunks.

step 3

Add the garlic and whole peppercorn. Cover and let it boil.

step 4

Reduce the heat to a simmer. Continue cooking until the pork tenderizes. Note: This should take around 60 minutes. You can add water as needed.

step 5

Add the remaining pineapple chunks and brown sugar. Season with salt as needed. Cook for 2 minutes.

step 6

Serve warm with rice. Share and enjoy!

ingredients

3 lbs. pork belly

3 cups pineapple juice

30 ounces pineapple chunks

9 cloves garlic

9 tablespoons soy sauce

4.5 tablespoons brown sugar

3 teaspoons whole peppercorn

4.5 tablespoons cooking oil

Salt to taste

tools

Large Pot

Sharp Knife

Chopping Board

Measuring Cups and Spoons

Wooden Spoon or Spatula

Serving Bowls
