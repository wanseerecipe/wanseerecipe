Chicken Fried Rice

Do you tend to cook more rice than you are able to finish? Filipinos love this side dish, and so sometimes, we underestimate just how easy it is to get full from one cup. Of course, you can always refrigerate your rice, and heat it up again the next day for your meals. But thatâ€™s just no fun, is it? If I were you, I would use that opportunity to create some delicious Chicken Fried Rice!

prep time

5 mins

cook time

20 mins

serving

3 servings

Tags : Chicken

instructions

step 1

Cut the chicken into small pieces. Arrange in a bowl and combine with the sauce ingredients. Mix well. Set aside.

step 2

Heat the oil in a pan. Fry the beaten eggs until done. Remove the cooked eggs from the pan. Chop it into pieces, and then set it aside.

step 3

Using the remaining oil (add more if needed), saute the garlic and onion.

step 4

Add the marinated chicken once the onion softens. Stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes.

step 5

Add the vegetables. Continue stir-frying for 2 minutes.

step 6

Add the rice. Toss until all the ingredients are well blended. Continue cooking for 3 minutes.

step 7

Season with salt and ground black pepper.

step 8

Transfer to a serving plate. Serve immediately.

ingredients

3 cups cooked white rice

2 eggs

6 ounces chicken breast

1 1/2 cups frozen mixed vegetables

1 onion

2 cloves garlic

3 tablespoons cooking oil

Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Sauce ingredients:

1 tablespoon oyster sauce

3 tablespoons soy sauce

1/2 teaspoon sesame oil

tools

Knife

Cutting board

Mixing bowl

Wok or Large Skillet

Ladle

Wooden spoon or spatula

Measuring cups and spoons
