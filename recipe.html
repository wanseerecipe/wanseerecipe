Dish Recipes

1. Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Cook pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Mix the egg and cheese mixture into the hot spaghetti.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

2. Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Procedure:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients without overmixing.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup or your favorite toppings.
