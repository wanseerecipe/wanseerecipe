Chop Suey

Chop Suey is a stir-fried vegetable dish that is cooked with meats such as chicken and pork. Shrimp and seafood can also be added. This chop suey recipe is special because it has almost every ingredient present. Chicken, pork, shrimp, and boiled quail eggs were all included in the recipe, along with fresh veggies.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

30 mins

serving

5 servings

Tags : vegetable fish

instructions

step 1

Pour the oil into the wok or frying pan, and let it heat.

step 2

Pan fry the shrimps for a minute on each side, take them out from the pan, and set aside.

step 3

Now, using the same hot oil, put the onion. When it becomes soft, add garlic and continue to saut√© until both are soft.

step 4

Add pork and chicken. Stir fry until light brown.

step 5

Add soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir.

step 6

Pour in some water; bring it to a boil, cover, and let it simmer for 15 minutes.

step 7

Add cauliflower, carrots, bell peppers, snow peas, and young corn. Stir.

step 8

Add cabbage. Toss. Cover and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.

step 9

Put the pan-fried shrimp into the pot and add ground black pepper.

step 10

Add the boiled quail eggs and cornstarch diluted in water. Toss.

step 11

Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.

step 12

Share and enjoy!

ingredients

7 pieces shrimp

3 ounces pork

3 ounces boneless chicken breast

1 1/2 cup cauliflower

1 piece carrot

15 pieces snow peas

8 pieces baby corn

1 piece red bell pepper

1 piece green bell pepper

1 1/2 cups cabbage

12 pieces quail eggs boiled

1 piece yellow onion

4 cloves garlic

1/4 cup soy sauce

1 1/2 tablespoons oyster sauce

3/4 cup water

1 tablespoon cornstarch

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

3 tablespoons cooking oil.

tools

Cutting Board

Knife

Wok
