Leche Flan

Leche Flan is a dessert made-up of eggs and milk with a soft caramel on top. It resembles crème caramel and caramel custard. This delicious dessert is known throughout the world.It has been a regular item in the menu of most restaurants because of its taste, ease in preparation and long shelf life. It can also be added as a component to build other great tasting dessert creations.

prep time

15 - 20 mins

cook time

30 - 35 mins

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dessert

instructions

step 1

Place a small pan over low heat, then melt ½ cup sugar until it becomes golden brown, caramelized.

step 2

Immediately pour the melted caramel into the llanera and tilt it so the bottom gets evenly coated. Allow it to harden.

step 3

In a mixing bowl, whisk egg yolks with condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

step 4

Pour the mixture of the flan through a strainer to remove lumps and bubbles.

step 5

Gently pour the strained mixture into the caramelized llanera.

step 6

Cover the llanera with foil and steam over low heat for 30-35 minutes.

step 7

Check with a toothpick, if it comes out clean it's done.

step 8

Leave the flan to cool at room temperature then refrigerate it for at least 3 hours.

step 9

Run a knife around the edges, place a plate on top, then flip the llanera to release the flan.

step 10

Serve immediately. Enjoy!

ingredients

5 egg yolks

1 1/2 cup condensed milk

1/2 cup evaporated milk

1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 1/2 cup white sugar

tools

Small llanera pan

Mixing bowl & whisk

Measuring Cup/Spoon

Strainer

Steamer with lid
