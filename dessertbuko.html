Buko Lychee Sorbet

Beat the heat with this refreshing and delicious frozen tropical treat! Buko Lychee Sorbet is super easy to make with only four ingredients and a great way to cool off in warmer weather.

prep time

5 mins

Freezing time

4 hrs

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dessert

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, combine shredded coconut, coconut juice, lychee (including its syrup), and sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved.

step 2

Transfer into a container with a tight-fitting lid and freeze for a few hours or until solid. Serve frozen.

ingredients

2 cups young coconut, shredded

2 cups coconut juice

1 can l(20 ounces) ychee in light syrup

2/3 cup sugar

tools

Serving Bowls

Spoons

Measuring Cup/Spoon
